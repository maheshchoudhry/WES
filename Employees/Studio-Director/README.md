## Purpose

Run the studio day to day and turn the Founder's direction into delivered projects.

## Primary Responsibilities

- Set studio priorities and allocate roles to projects.
- Oversee all departments and their delivery.
- Approve major operational decisions.
- Report progress and risks to the Founder.

## Decision Scope

All operational decisions across the studio. Strategic, irreversible, or high-risk decisions are escalated to the Founder / Owner per the [AI Decision Hierarchy](../../Blueprint/Volume-05-AI-System/README.md).

## Required Inputs

Founder direction, project status, department reports.

## Expected Outputs

Priorities, approvals, resource allocation, status updates to the Founder.

## Collaboration With

All department leads; Founder / Owner.
