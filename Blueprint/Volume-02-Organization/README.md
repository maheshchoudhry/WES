# Volume 02 — Organization

**Version:** v1.0 &nbsp;|&nbsp; **Status:** Draft

This volume defines how WORLD Engineering Studio (WES) is organized: its structure, departments, reporting lines, and its AI workforce.

---

## Company Structure

WES operates as a lean, department-based studio. A human **Founder/Owner** sets direction; a **Studio Director** (AI) runs day-to-day operations; departments deliver the work. The structure is designed to scale from one project to many without changing its core shape.

```
Founder / Owner (Human)
        |
  Studio Director (AI)
        |
  ┌─────┴───────────────────────────────────────┐
Product &   Engineering   AI Systems   Quality &   Project Mgmt   Knowledge &
 Design                                 Security    & Operations   Documentation
```

## Departments

| # | Department | Focus |
|---|------------|-------|
| 1 | **Product & Design** | Defines what to build and how it should look and feel. |
| 2 | **Engineering** | Designs architecture and builds the software. |
| 3 | **AI Systems** | Builds and integrates AI capabilities into projects. |
| 4 | **Quality & Security** | Ensures work is correct, safe, and reliable. |
| 5 | **Project Management & Operations** | Plans, tracks, and keeps delivery on schedule. |
| 6 | **Knowledge & Documentation** | Captures knowledge and maintains documentation. |

## Reporting Hierarchy

```
Founder / Owner
└─ Studio Director
   ├─ Product Manager

   ├─ Software Architect
   │  ├─ Frontend Engineer
   │  ├─ Backend Engineer
   │  ├─ AI Engineer
   │  │  └─ Prompt Engineer
   │  └─ DevOps / Automation Engineer
   ├─ QA Engineer
   ├─ Security Engineer
   └─ Project Manager
      └─ Technical Writer
```

Full role definitions are in [Volume 03 — Roles](../Volume-03-Roles/README.md).

## AI Employee Overview

Every role in WES is performed by an **AI employee** — a defined agent with a clear purpose, responsibilities, and reporting line. AI employees:

- Operate within the standards defined by the Blueprint.
- Collaborate across departments on shared projects.
- Are accountable to their reporting role for quality and delivery.
- Can be added or scaled as project needs grow.

The human Founder/Owner retains final authority and strategic direction.
