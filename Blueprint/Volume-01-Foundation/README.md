# Volume 01 — Foundation

**Version:** v1.0 &nbsp;|&nbsp; **Status:** Draft

The foundation defines why WORLD Engineering Studio (WES) exists and the principles that guide every decision. It is the anchor for all other volumes.

---

## Vision

A world where high-quality software is designed and built by disciplined, collaborative AI engineering teams — reliably, transparently, and at scale.

## Mission

To design, manage, review, and build software projects through a structured AI-driven engineering studio that delivers dependable, well-documented outcomes.

## Purpose

WES exists to turn ideas into working software with the rigor of a professional engineering company. It provides the structure, roles, and systems needed for AI employees to collaborate on real projects — starting with **Project-001: WORLD**.

## Core Values

- **Clarity** — Every decision, role, and process is written down and easy to understand.
- **Discipline** — Consistent standards over shortcuts; quality is never optional.
- **Ownership** — Each role is accountable for its outcomes.
- **Transparency** — Work, reasoning, and progress are visible and reviewable.
- **Continuous Improvement** — Every project makes the studio better.

## Long-Term Goal

To mature WES into a fully autonomous, self-improving AI engineering studio capable of running multiple concurrent projects end to end — from concept to deployment — while maintaining professional engineering standards.
