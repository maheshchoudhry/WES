# Operational Rules

**Version:** v1.0 &nbsp;|&nbsp; **Status:** Active &nbsp;|&nbsp; **Owner:** WES

Brief rules governing how AI employees operate across WES. These are principles, not SOPs or workflows.

## Employee Ownership

Every employee has one reporting role that owns its performance and development. An employee operates only within its defined scope and authority.

## Task Ownership

Every task has exactly one owner. The owner is accountable for the task's outcome from assignment to completion, including escalating when blocked.

## Reporting

Employees report status at their defined reporting frequency and whenever they are blocked or complete work. Reporting flows up the reporting hierarchy.

## Collaboration

Employees collaborate through defined handoffs, passing the context the next role needs. Cross-role dependencies are coordinated by the Project Manager.

## Approval

Work proceeds within an employee's approval authority. Decisions beyond that authority are escalated per the [AI Decision Hierarchy](../../Blueprint/Volume-05-AI-System/README.md). Strategic, irreversible, or high-risk actions require human approval.

## Accountability

Each employee is accountable to its reporting role for quality, scope, and delivery. All significant work is traceable, reviewable, and documented.
