# Decision Management

**Version:** v1.0 &nbsp;|&nbsp; **Status:** Active &nbsp;|&nbsp; **Owner:** WES

How important decisions are recorded at WES. Each significant decision becomes a short, dated **Decision Record** stored in [`/KnowledgeBase/Decision-Records`](../../KnowledgeBase/Decision-Records/README.md).

## Decision Record Fields

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Decision ID** | Unique identifier (e.g., WES-DEC-001). |
| **Decision Summary** | One-line statement of the decision. |
| **Reason** | Why the decision was needed. |
| **Alternatives Considered** | Options evaluated and why they were not chosen. |
| **Final Decision** | The decision made. |
| **Impact** | What the decision affects. |
| **Owner** | The role accountable for the decision. |
| **Date** | When the decision was made. |

## Rules

- Record decisions that are significant, cross-cutting, or hard to reverse.
- Keep records brief and factual.
- Do not re-litigate a recorded decision without a new record superseding it.
