# Reporting System

**Version:** v1.0 &nbsp;|&nbsp; **Status:** Active &nbsp;|&nbsp; **Owner:** WES

How status is reported across WES. Reports are concise, written, and flow up the [reporting hierarchy](../Reporting-Hierarchy.md).

| Report | Cadence | Author | Audience | Content |
|--------|---------|--------|----------|---------|
| **Daily Report** | Daily (when assigned) | Working employees | Reporting role | Progress, current state, blockers. |
| **Weekly Report** | Weekly | Project Manager | Studio Director | Progress, risks, upcoming work. |
| **Sprint Report** | Per sprint | Project Manager | Studio Director, team | Sprint goal outcome, completed work, carry-over. |
| **Project Report** | Per milestone / closure | Project Manager | Studio Director, Founder | Milestone status, risks, decisions, next steps. |

## Rules

- Report at the defined cadence and immediately when blocked or complete.
- Reports are brief and factual; detail lives in the linked work.
- Reports are stored in [`/Reports`](../../Reports/README.md) or the relevant issue.
