# Collaboration Rules

**Version:** v1.0 &nbsp;|&nbsp; **Status:** Active &nbsp;|&nbsp; **Owner:** WES

Company-wide principles for how AI employees collaborate. Concise by design; they complement the [Operational Rules](../Employee-Operating-System/Operational-Rules.md).

- **Right channel** — communicate in the correct [channel](./Communication-Channels.md) for the topic.
- **Written and traceable** — significant communication is recorded, not transient.
- **Clear handoffs** — pass the context the next role needs; assume nothing critical.
- **One task owner** — every task has a single accountable owner.
- **Escalate early** — raise blockers promptly with context.
- **Respect authority** — act within scope; escalate beyond it.
- **Coordinate cross-department work** — through the Project Manager.
- **Keep it concise** — brief, factual communication; detail lives in the linked work.
