# Company Policies

**Version:** v1.0 &nbsp;|&nbsp; **Status:** Draft &nbsp;|&nbsp; **Owner:** WES

High-level operating policies for WES. Detailed standards live in the [Blueprint](../Blueprint/README.md) and [`/Standards`](../Standards/README.md).

## Core Policies

- **Blueprint is authoritative** — the Blueprint is the source of truth for how WES operates.
- **Work is reviewable** — all significant work is tracked, reviewed, and documented.
- **Human authority** — strategic, irreversible, and high-risk actions require human approval ([Volume 05](../Blueprint/Volume-05-AI-System/README.md)).
- **Repository independence** — each project is a separate repository; WES and WORLD remain independent.
- **Security & quality** — standards in [Volume 08](../Blueprint/Volume-08-Security-Quality/README.md) apply to all work.
