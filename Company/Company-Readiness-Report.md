## Organizational Readiness

**Status: READY (core organization).**

- Every department is staffed with at least one active employee.
- Every role has a defined purpose, authority level, decision scope, inputs, outputs, and reporting line.
- Reporting hierarchy and escalation path are complete and consistent with the Blueprint.
- The company can plan and coordinate work end to end at a structural level.

The organization is structurally complete for a single project. It is **not yet execution-ready** because operating instructions (SOPs and prompts) are intentionally out of scope for this phase.

## Missing Capabilities

Gaps to close before full operational execution:

- **Operating instructions** — no SOPs or role prompts yet (needed to make employees executable).
- **Single-person departments** — Leadership and Knowledge & Documentation have no backup/redundancy.
- **Specialist roles not yet hired** — e.g., Data Engineer, ML Ops, UX Researcher, QA Automation Specialist, Release Manager.
- **Orchestration / automation layer** — coordination between employees is manual (future scope, [Volume 10](../Blueprint/Volume-10-Automation/README.md)).
- **Standards & templates** — currently placeholders in [`/Standards`](../Standards/README.md) and [`/Templates`](../Templates/README.md).

## Recommended Next Hiring Phase

**Phase 6 — Specialist Activation (deferred until Project-001 scope is defined):**

- Hire specialists **only as project needs demand**, to avoid over-staffing.
- Likely first specialists: Data Engineer and QA Automation Specialist, once WORLD's technical scope is known.
- Add redundancy for single-person departments as workload grows.
- Precede execution with an **operating-instructions phase** (SOPs and prompts per role) rather than more hiring.

---

## Conclusion

WES has a **complete, active core organization**: 13 AI employees across 6 departments with clear roles, authority, and reporting lines. The studio is **organizationally ready to begin operational onboarding**. The recommended next step is to define operating instructions per role before beginning Project-001 execution.
